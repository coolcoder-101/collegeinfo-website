Welcome to the vibrant and dynamic Mumbai campus of Liberty University. Nestled in the heart of India's financial capital, our Mumbai campus offers a world-class education in a bustling metropolis known for its rich culture, thriving industries, and endless opportunities. As an integral part of the university, our Mumbai campus is dedicated to providing a diverse and inclusive learning environment, where students from all backgrounds come together to pursue their academic dreams. With state-of-the-art facilities, renowned faculty, and a commitment to innovation, our Mumbai campus is a hub for excellence in education, preparing students for successful careers in a globalized world.
